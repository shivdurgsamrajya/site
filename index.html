Exploring the Rich History of Maharashtra Forts

Raigad Fort

by Admin

Raigad, situated in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India, is a hill fort located in the town of Mahad. It is one of the strongest fortresses on the Deccan Plateau and was historically referred to as Rairee or Rairy fort

Pratapgad Fort History

Pratapgad is a mountain fort located in Satara district, in the Western Indian state of Maharashtra. The fort is situated 24 kilometres from the Mahabaleshwar hill station. The fort is now a popular tourist destination.

Sinhgad (Kondhana) Fort History

Sinhgad (Kondhana) Fort Maharashtra : Sinhagad is an ancient hill fortress located at around 49 km southwest of the city of Pune, India. Previously known as Kondhana, the fort had been the site of many battles, most notably the Battle of Sinhagad in 1670.

Tikona Fort History

Tikona Fort Maharashtra : Tikona Fort, also known as Vitandgad, is a historic hill fort located in the Maval region of Maharashtra, India. It is a popular trekking destination and offers a glimpse into the rich history and natural beauty of the area.

Tung Fort History

Tikona Fort Maharashtra : Tung Fort, also known as Kathingad Fort, is another historic hill fort located in the Pune district of Maharashtra, India. Like Sinhagad and Tikona Forts, Tung Fort has a rich history and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.
